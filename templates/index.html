Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Quick answers about the permission request process.

Who can approve my Outpass request?

Your request will be routed automatically to your assigned Faculty Advisor or the Head of Department (HOD) for review and approval, based on the current campus policy.

How long does the approval process usually take?

Most requests are reviewed within 4 to 6 hours during working days. You will receive an instant notification on your dashboard or via email once the status changes.

Can I cancel a submitted permission request?

Yes, you can cancel a request only if its status is still Pending. Once approved or rejected, the request cannot be modified. Look for the 'Cancel Request' button on your dashboard's status page.

What should I do if my request is rejected?

If your request is rejected, please check the rejection reason provided by the faculty. You can resubmit a new request with revised dates or a more detailed explanation of the need.
